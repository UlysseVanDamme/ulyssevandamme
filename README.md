## 🎵 About Me

> *"Where music theory meets machine learning."*

I'm a developer at the crossroads of **music** and **artificial intelligence** — fascinated by what happens when deep learning listens to Bach, Chopin, or Beethoven.

- 🎹 **Piano player** turned data scientist
- 🤖 Building ML models that **understand music structure**
- 🔬 Exploring **Transformer architectures** for symbolic music
- ⚡ Implementing **neural networks from scratch** in C because why not
- 🎓 Diving deep into Goodfellow's *Deep Learning* book
